Bio

Ben is currently the Director of Institutional Advancement at the Coalition of Sites of Conscience, where he leads all aspects of the Coalition’s fundraising efforts by developing, implementing, and managing short- and long-term fundraising plans to grow and diversify the Coalition’s base of funding. As a member of the senior leadership team, Ben is also integral to shaping the strategic direction for the organization. Ben’s extensive experience in development for international non-profits includes working at The Hunger Project, Trickle Up, and FilmAid International before joining the Coalition in 2011. In previous positions, he has focused on raising funds from institutions in the U.S., Europe, Australia, Africa, and Asia. Ben developed a strong interest in international development and human rights after traveling to Haiti as a teenager, and his later travels have included rural Guatemala, Ghana, India, and a winter teaching English in the Shandong Province of northern China.
